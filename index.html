Help App Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2, 2026

Help App ("the Application") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Information We Collect

The Application may collect the following information:

  • Telegram ID for authentication.
  • Device location (only with your permission).
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token for push notifications.
  • Application version and limited technical device information required for proper operation.

The Application does not collect passwords, banking information, or payment data.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to:

  • Authenticate users through Telegram.
  • Provide emergency assistance functionality.
  • Display and transmit location when requested by the user.
  • Deliver push notifications.
  • Improve application stability and security.

3. Location Data

Location access is required for the core functionality of the Application.

The Application may:

  • Determine your current location.
  • Display your position on the map.
  • Send your location to the server and authorized users when an emergency request is initiated.

Location data is processed only after you grant the required permissions. You may revoke location permission at any time through your device settings.

4. Push Notifications

The Application uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send emergency alerts and important notifications.

5. Data Sharing

The Application does not sell or rent personal information.

Information is shared only when necessary to provide application functionality, including communication with the backend server and Firebase notification services.

6. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect collected information against unauthorized access, modification, disclosure, or destruction.

7. Third-Party Services

The Application may use the following third-party services:

  • Telegram (user authentication)
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (push notifications)
  • OpenStreetMap (map services)

These services operate according to their own privacy policies.

8. Your Rights

You may:

  • Withdraw location permission at any time.
  • Stop using the Application whenever you wish.
  • Request deletion of your account and related data by contacting the developer.
